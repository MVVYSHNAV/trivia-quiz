About

Welcome to our school's annual Trivia Quiz! This event is designed to challenge students' knowledge across various subjects, including history, science, literature, and more. It's a fun and engaging way to promote learning and critical thinking among our students.

Participants will answer a series of multiple-choice questions, with each question offering four possible answers. The quiz is structured to cater to different grade levels, ensuring that everyone can participate and enjoy the experience. Whether you're a trivia novice or a seasoned expert, there's something here for everyone.

The Trivia Quiz is a great opportunity for students to showcase their knowledge, compete with peers, and win exciting prizes. We encourage all students to join in, test their skills, and most importantly, have fun!

Good luck, and may the best team win!
